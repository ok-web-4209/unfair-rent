ABOUT DAVID HOFFMAN

A Voice for Tenants.
A Force for Justice.

David Hoffman didn’t become a tenant rights attorney by accident — he chose this path because he believes every renter in Florida deserves to be treated with fairness and dignity. After witnessing far too many landlords exploit hard-working tenants by withholding security deposits without legal justification, David founded Hoffman Legal with a singular mission: to level the playing field.

With over a decade of dedicated practice in Florida landlord-tenant law, David has recovered millions of dollars in wrongfully withheld security deposits for thousands of tenants throughout the state. His approach is equal parts compassion and tenacity — he listens to every client’s story with genuine care, then fights relentlessly to ensure justice is served.

David’s philosophy is simple: no tenant should have to walk away from money that rightfully belongs to them simply because they feel outmatched by a landlord or property management company. Under his leadership, Hoffman Legal operates on a no-fee-unless-we-win basis, ensuring that access to justice is never limited by financial means.

Whether you’re a first-time renter in Miami or a long-time tenant in Tallahassee, David and his team extend the same unwavering commitment to every corner of the Sunshine State. He is fluent in both English and Spanish, and prides himself on making legal representation accessible, transparent, and empowering for all.

When you work with David, you’re not just hiring an attorney — you’re gaining an advocate who genuinely believes that justice should never depend on the size of your wallet. He answers his clients’ questions personally, explains every step in plain language, and treats each case with the urgency and respect it deserves.
