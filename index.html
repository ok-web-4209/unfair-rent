MEET YOUR ATTORNEY

David Hoffman, Esq.

Founder & Lead Tenant Rights Attorney

David Hoffman is a Florida-licensed attorney who has built his entire practice around one mission: making sure renters across the Sunshine State get every dollar they are legally owed when a landlord wrongfully withholds a security deposit. With over a decade of focused experience in Florida landlord-tenant law and Florida Statute 83.49, David has personally guided thousands of clients to successful recoveries.

What sets David apart is the way he combines deep legal knowledge with genuine compassion. He answers his clients’ calls personally, explains the law in plain English, and treats every case — whether it’s for $300 or $30,000 — with the same urgency and respect. His firm operates on a no-fee-unless-we-win basis, so financial pressure never stands between a tenant and the justice they deserve.

David is fluent in both English and Spanish, and he serves clients in every corner of Florida. Hablamos español.
