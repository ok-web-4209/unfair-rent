Serving Tenants Statewide

From our office in Dania Beach, Hoffman Legal helps renters recover unfairly withheld security deposits under Florida Statute 83.49. The same statewide law protects every Florida tenant — the 15-day refund deadline, the 30-day notice rule for deductions, the ban on charging ordinary wear and tear, and the forfeiture penalty when landlords get it wrong. What changes from place to place is the local picture: which county courthouse hears your case, how the area’s landlords and management companies operate, and the deduction patterns we see again and again in each market.
